Built specifically for XRP compliance

Every feature is engineered for the unique risk topology of the XRP Ledger and its growing ecosystem of bridges and sidechains.

01

XRPL native transaction graph

We trace XRP payments, trust lines, offers, escrows and payment channels natively on the XRPL — not just simple account transfers — reconstructing laundering paths up to 15 hops deep across the full ledger history.

02

Cross-chain bridge monitoring

Detect XRP moved via bridges to Ethereum, BNB Chain or Avalanche — a growing layering technique. Our engine links on-chain bridge events to XRPL source wallets that generic AML tools never connect.

03

XRPL DEX & AMM risk scoring

The XRP Ledger has a built-in decentralized exchange and AMM pools. We score counterparty exposure from DEX trades and AMM swaps — essential for OTC desks and payment providers using XRPL liquidity.

04

OFAC & FinCEN real-time sync

We sync OFAC SDN, FinCEN, EU, UN, OFSI and 30+ national sanctions lists in real time with no delay. XRPL-linked addresses from enforcement actions are added within minutes of publication.

Ripple ODL corridor analysis

On-Demand Liquidity (ODL) flows move XRP through market makers across corridors like USD→MXN. We score the full corridor chain — not just the originating wallet — for complete remittance compliance.

06

Automated SAR generation

Enterprise plans auto-generate pre-filled Suspicious Activity Reports formatted for FinCEN, AUSTRAC, EU AMLD6 and FCA — reducing compliance team workload by up to 80% for XRP-related filings.

Risk Classification

Understanding your XRP risk score

Our 0–100 scoring model follows FATF risk-based approach guidelines and is calibrated on real XRPL enforcement cases.

Low Risk · 0–25

Safe to transact

No direct or indirect exposure to sanctioned addresses, darknet markets or known fraud wallets detected within a 10-hop XRPL radius.

  • No OFAC or FinCEN match
  • Clean XRPL transaction graph
  • Recognised exchange counterparties
  • Normal XRPL velocity patterns
Medium Risk · 26–74

Manual review required

Indirect exposure detected through unhosted wallets, high-frequency DEX trading patterns or connections to grey-market OTC desks. Enhanced due diligence required.

  • Indirect risky address proximity
  • Unusual XRPL DEX trading patterns
  • Unlicensed exchange interactions
  • Abnormal escrow usage patterns
High Risk · 75–100

Block or file SAR

Direct exposure to OFAC-sanctioned addresses, darknet market wallets, confirmed ransomware addresses or multiple high-risk ODL corridor counterparties detected.

  • OFAC SDN or FinCEN direct match
  • Darknet market direct exposure
  • Confirmed illicit ODL corridor hop
  • Multiple high-risk counterparties
